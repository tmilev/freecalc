Solve the inequality.
\[
\begin{array}{rclll}
\alertNoH{2,3}{2x^2+3x-5}  &\geq &0\\
\uncover<2->{(\fcAnswer{3}{\alertNoH{4, 9, 10, 15, 16, 21, 22}{ 2x+5} })( \fcAnswer{3}{ \alertNoH{5, 11, 12, 17, 18, 23, 24}{x-1}} )&\geq &0}\\
\uncover<28->{\alertNoH{28}{x \in} \fcAnswer{29}{\left(-\infty, -\frac{5}{2} \right] \cup [1,\infty)}}
\end{array}
\]
\uncover<4->{Left hand side vanishes when $\alertNoH{4}{x=-\frac{5}{2}}$ and when $\alertNoH{5}{x=1}$. } \uncover<6->{The two roots split the real line into three intervals: $ \alertNoH{6}{\left(-\infty,-\frac{5}{2} \right)}, \alertNoH{7}{ \left(-\frac{5}{2},1\right)},\alertNoH{8}{ \left(1, \infty \right)}$. }
