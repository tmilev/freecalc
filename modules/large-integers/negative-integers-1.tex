\frametitle{Negative integers}
\begin{itemize}
\item The negative integers are the numbers:
\[
\dots, -6, -5, -4, -3, -2, -1
\]
\item<2-> Written as the minus sign $-$ followed by a (positive) number.

\item<3-> \alertNoH{4}{Negatives} are to the \alertNoH{4}{left of $0$} on the \alertNoH{3}{number line}.
