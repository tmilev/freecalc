Find the distance between $(-2,3)$ and $(3,5)$.

$d=\sqrt{(x_1-x_2)^2+(y_1 -y_2)^2}= \sqrt{(-2-3)^2+ (3-5)^2} = \sqrt{(-5)^2+(-2)^2}= \sqrt{25+4}=\sqrt{29}\approx 5.385$.

\end{example}
