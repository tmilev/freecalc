$$d(A,B) = |AB| = \sqrt{(x_B-x_A)^2+(y_B-y_A)^2+(z_B-z_A)^2}$$
%
Example: $P(3,1,2)$ and $Q(1,2,3)$:\pause
%
$$D(P,Q) = \sqrt{(1-3)^2+(2-1)^2+(3-2)^2} = \sqrt{6}\; .$$
