Computation shows that 
$\displaystyle
f'(x)=\frac{- x^{2}+3 x-\frac{3}{2} }{ \left(x^2- 3 x+3\right)^2}
$
and that 
$\displaystyle f''(x)=\frac{(2x-3)x(x-3)}{\left(x^2- 3 x+3\right)^3}$; you may use those computations without further justification.
