\begin{example}
Find the volume of the parallelepiped with vertex at the origin and spanned by $\fcv v_1=(1,2,3)$, $\fcv v_2=(3,5,7)$, $\fcv v_3=(5,7,11)$.
\[
\Vol_3=\left| 
\det \left(\begin{array}{ccc} 1& 2& 3\\
3& 5& 7\\ 
5& 7& 11 \end{array}\right)\right|=|-2|=2.
\]
\end{example}
