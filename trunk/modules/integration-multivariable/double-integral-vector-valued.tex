\frametitle{Vectorial Integrals}
The double integral definition extends directly to f-ns with vector output.
\begin{definition}
\[
\iint_{\mathcal{R}} \fcv{F}(P) \; \diff A = \lim_{\text{maxdiam}(\mathcal{D}) \to 0} \sum_k \fcv{F}(P_k) \; \text{area}(D_k)
\]
\end{definition}
