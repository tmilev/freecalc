Let $S$ be an oriented surface with orientation given by the \alert<2>{unit normal field $\fcv N$}. Let \alert<3>{$\fcv{F}$ be a smooth vector field on $S$}.
\begin{definition}
The flux of $\fcv{F}$ across $S$ is
\[
\iint_S \alert<4>{\fcv{F}\cdot \fcv{N}} \diff S = \iint_S \fcv{F} \cdot \fcv{\diff S} .
\]
\end{definition}
