\answer{ $\Delta x = 1$ and $f(x) = \frac{1}{x^2+1}$. Thus $\displaystyle \int \limits_{-3.5}^{-0.5} f(x) \diff x  \approx \Delta x\left(f{} \left(-3 \right)+ f{}\left( -2\right)+f{}\left(-1\right)\right)=\frac{4}{5}=0.8$.}
