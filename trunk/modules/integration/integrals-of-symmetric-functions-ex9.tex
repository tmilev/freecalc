\begin{example}[Example 10, p. 381]
Since $f(x) = (\tan x)/ (1 + x^2 + x^4) $ satisfies $f(-x) = -f(x)$, it is odd, and so
\abovedisplayskip=0pt
\belowdisplayskip=0pt
\begin{align*}
\int_{-1}^1 \frac{\tan x}{1+x^2+x^4} \diff x%
& = %
\uncover<2->{%
0.%
}%
\end{align*}
\end{example}
