Express as the following as an algebraic expression of $x$. In other words, ``get rid'' of the trigonometric and inverse trigonometric expressions.
\begin{multicols}{2}
\begin{enumerate}
\item $\cos^2(\Arctan x)$. \answer{$\frac{1}{1+x^2} $}
\item $-\sin^2(\Arccot x)$. \answer{ $-\frac{1}{1+x^2}$}
\item $\frac{1}{\cos(\Arcsin x)}$. \answer{$\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}}$}
\item $-\frac{1}{\sin(\Arccos x)}$.\answer{$-\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}}$}
\end{enumerate}
\end{multicols}
