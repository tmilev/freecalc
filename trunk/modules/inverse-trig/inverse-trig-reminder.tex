\begin{frame}
All of the inverse trigonometric derivatives also give rise to integration formulas.  These two are the most important:
\begin{align*}
\int \frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}}\diff x & = \Arcsin x + C.\\
& \\
\int \frac{1}{x^2 + 1}\diff x & = \Arctan x + C.
\end{align*}
\end{frame}
