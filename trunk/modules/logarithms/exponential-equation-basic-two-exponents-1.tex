Solve the equation.
\[\renewcommand{\arraystretch}{1.7}
\begin{array}{rcll|l}
\displaystyle e^{x-3}&=&\displaystyle 2 e^{2x-1}&&\text{Divide by } e^{2x-1}\\
\displaystyle \frac{e^{x-3}}{e^{2x-1}}&=&2\\
\displaystyle e^{x-3-(2x-1)} &=& 2\\
\displaystyle e^{-x-2} &=& 2&&\text{Apply }\ln\\
-x-2&=&\ln 2\\
x&=&-(\ln 2+2)\\
x&=&-\ln 2-2
\end{array}
\]
\end{example}
