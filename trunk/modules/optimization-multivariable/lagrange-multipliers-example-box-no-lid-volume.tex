Find the maximal volume of a box with no lid whose surface area is $10m^2$.

\uncover<2->{ Let the three dimensions of the box be $\alert<2>{ x,y,z}$.} 
\end{columns}
\uncover<3->{We seek to maximize $V=xyz$.
The restriction is: $g(x,y,z)=xy+2(zx+zy)-10=0 $. By the Lagrange multiplier's method, we need to solve the system:
\[
 \nabla V= \langle yz, zx, xy \rangle =\lambda \nabla g=\lambda \langle2 z+y, 2 z+x, 2 y+2 x \rangle
\]
