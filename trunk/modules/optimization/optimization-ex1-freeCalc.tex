A cone is folded from a wedge-shaped profile of radius $r$. Find the maximal possible volume of such a cone.

\begin{columns}[c]
\column{.3\textwidth}

\rput[b] (0.5,1){$B$}
\rput[b] (0.15,0.4){$r$}
\rput[b] (-0.5,1){$A$}
\end{pspicture}

\psset{xunit=1cm, yunit=1cm}

\tiny 
\psparametricplot[algebraic,linewidth=1.5pt,linecolor=red]{0}{5}{2*sqrt(t)|cos(t)+t}
\psline (-2,0)(0, 2)(2,0)

\end{pspicture} 

\column{.7\textwidth}
Let $V$ - volume of cone, $B$ - area base, $h$ - height. Then $V=\frac13 Bh$.
