Find $\int \frac{x^2+2x-1}{2x^3+3x^2-2x}\diff x$.
\begin{itemize}
\item<2->  deg$(x^2+2x-1) < $ deg$(2x^3+3x^2-2x)$: don't divide.
\item<3->  Factor denominator: $2x^3 + 3x^2-2x = x(2x-1)(x+2)$.
\end{itemize}
