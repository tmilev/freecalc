$\begin{array}{rcl}
z&=&0\\
\left(\frac{x}{2}\right)^2+\left(\frac{y}{\sqrt{2}}\right)^2&=& \onlyNoH{3}{ 0} \onlyNoH{5}{1} \onlyNoH{7}{2^2=4}\onlyNoH{9}{3^2=9} \\
\uncover<3,4|handout:0>{\Rightarrow (x,y,z)&=&(0,0,0)}\\
\uncover<5-|handout:0>{\Rightarrow (x,y,z)&\in&\text{ellipse}}\\
\end{array}
$ 

\column{0.6\textwidth}
\begin{itemize}
\item Consider the surface 
$\mathcal C =\left\{(x,y,z) |\frac{x^2}{4}+\frac{y^2}{2}=z^2 \right\}$ 
\item<2-> The level curves $z=const$ are:
\begin{itemize}
\item<3-> A point for $z=0$.
\item<4-> Ellipses for $z\neq 0$.
\end{itemize}

\item<9-> Figure is called a cone.
\end{itemize}
