\begin{itemize}
\item Consider the surface 
$\mathcal C =\left\{(x,y,z) |\frac{x^2}{4}+\frac{y^2}{2}=z^2 \right\}$ 
\item<2-> The level curves $z=const$ are:
\begin{itemize}
\item<4-> A point for $z=0$.
\item<6-> Ellipses for $z\neq 0$.
\end{itemize}

\item<9-> Figure is called a cone.
\end{itemize}
