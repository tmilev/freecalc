Is the sequence $a_n = (-1)^n$ convergent or divergent?

\begin{itemize}
\item<2->  The terms oscillate between $-1$ and $1$ infinitely many times.
\item<3->  Therefore the sequence doesn't approach any number.
\item<4->  $\{ a_n\}$ is divergent.
\end{itemize}
