This theorem summarizes the results of the previous example.
\begin{theorem}[$p$-series Convergence]
The $p$-series $\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^\infty \frac{1}{n^p}$ is convergent if $p > 1$ and divergent if $p \leq 1$.
\end{theorem}
