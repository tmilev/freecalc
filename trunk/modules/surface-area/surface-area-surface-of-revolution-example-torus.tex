\begin{example}
Use Pappus' theorem to find the surfacea area of a torus of major radius $R$ and minor radius $r$.

\uncover<2->{
For a torus

the length of the revolved circle is $2\pi r$;

the centroid is at $(R,0)$;

hence the surface area of a torus is $4\pi^2 Rr$.
}
