\item<handout:2-|15->  The circular cross-section through the \alertNoH{15}{point $(x, 0)$} has \alertNoH{16}{radius $\sqrt{x}$}.

\item<handout:3|19->  The volume of a single approximating section is $\alertNoH{20}{ A(x)  \Delta x }$.
